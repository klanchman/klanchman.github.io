Who I Am

Software Developer

I'm a software developer, and I love what I do both at work, and in my free time. I like to have a couple of personal side projects at any given time. Right now, mobile development is something that I am focusing on. I'm a strong believer that mobile devices will only become more prevalent than they already are, and that the importance of creating great software for these devices will only become more important as time goes on.

Besides software development, I'm interested in learning more about user interface and graphic design. I really enjoy applications that have a clean and functional interface. One goal I have is to be able to design UIs that I would want to use. I'm also really interested in system administration. I like to keep everything on my home network running smoothly, and I'd love to do similar work on a larger scale.

Tech Enthusiast

I love just about anything related to technology. I'm always reading about new technologies and tools, and whenever I have time I enjoy learning about and using ones that I'm really interested in.

Warranty Voider

Simply put, I like to tinker. I've voided the warranty on nearly every device I've owned, usually through software modifications. (Of course, I'm always cautious enough to prevent ruining my devices.) Early on, I did what I could with what I had. An MP3 player, for instance: I flashed a customizable 3rd party firmware on it. I went on to root every Android device that I've owned. Most of the time, I ended up flashing custom ROMs on those, too. I love to experiment with my devices to see what I can do with them. It's a hobby, really.

Writer, Photographer, and Musician?

Well, kinda. I have some other interests outside of the realm of technology. I really enjoy writing; I keep a small blog that I update occasionally when I think of something I want to write about. Unfortunately, that doesn't happen as much as I'd like. I also enjoy photography, though I'm not particularly good at it. I want to find some more time to devote to that. I also enjoy music, both listening to it and playing it. I have an electronic drum set that I'm learning to play and having a lot of fun with. Hopefully I can get an acoustic set at some point...
